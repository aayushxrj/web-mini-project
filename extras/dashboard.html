    Lagori

    The game Lagori or seven stones is a Traditional Indian fun game originated in some parts of present day Karnataka, and it is known with different names in different states and played across India. For example, its called LAGORI in Karnataka, pittu in Andhra Pradesh, Ezhu Kallu in Tamil Nadu, Lingot in Telangana, Lingorcha in Maharashtra, pithu in central and northern parts of India.


    Price: ₹300

    Riviera Fashion Week

    Riviera Fashion Week is a unique, one-day event emphasizing sustainability in fashion. It features two main rounds: the Design Round and the Runway Round. In the Design Round, participants showcase their creativity by designing dresses highlighting the importance of reusability in fashion. The Runway Round is a dynamic ramp walk where these designs are brought to life, demonstrating how sustainable practices can be integrated into high fashion.
