    • Frequently asked questions

    • What is Aarambh 2024?

      Aarambh 2024 is a virtual technical conference, our annual marquee product, and community event featuring technical product experts, software leaders, and enterprise teams who are building the world’s most important technologies. It’s a chance for our global interconnected community to come together and share best practices, learn from each other, and hear about the newest products and features.

    • How can I participate in Aarambh 2024?

      Experience Aarambh 2024 to the fullest with a range of activities: Watch the opening keynote, join breakout sessions in any of our four channels, participate in conversations and ask questions on GitHub Discussions, download additional resources provided by speakers, enjoy live creative coding performances and tutorials on the Play channel, design your own Octocat, buy some cool Aarambh swag (and support great causes), network and connect at an Orbital event around Aarambh, and check out our sponsor demos.

    • How do I log on?

      To watch any of the live streaming content, simply go to aarambh2024.com, at the date and time of the broadcast. Comments and questions will take place on GitHub Discussions, which requires a (free) GitHub account. If you register for workshops, we will provide details about logging in and participating.
